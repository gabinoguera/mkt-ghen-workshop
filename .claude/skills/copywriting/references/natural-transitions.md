## Best Practices

- **Match tone to audience** — Casual transitions for casual copy
- **Vary your transitions** — Don't use the same one twice in a section
- **Front-load** — Put the important information first, transition second
- **Don't over-signpost** — Sometimes the best transition is no transition

## Phrases to Avoid (AI Tells)

- "It's worth noting that..."
- "In today's digital landscape..."
- "Dive deep into..."
- "At the end of the day..."
- "Unlock the power of..."
- "In an era of..."
- "Navigate the complexities of..."
- "Leverage the potential of..."
- "Seamlessly integrate..."
- "Harness the power of..."
